About Spin N Win

Welcome to Spin N Win, a fun wheel-spinning game where you can test your luck! This game was built using Phaser.js, a powerful HTML5 game framework.

How to play: Simply click or tap anywhere on the screen to spin the wheel. The wheel will rotate and eventually stop on one of the segments. Each segment contains a different prize or outcome.

Possible outcomes include doubling your money, getting another spin, or losing. The game is designed to be simple yet entertaining. Enjoy spinning the wheel and good luck!
