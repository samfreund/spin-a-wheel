About Spin N Win

Welcome to Spin N Win, a fun wheel-spinning game where you can test your luck! This game was built using Phaser.js, a powerful HTML5 game framework.

How to play: Simply click or tap anywhere on the screen to spin the wheel.

The amount bet is up to the player, it can be as little or as much as they would like.

Side bets are also accepted, which are to be placed on the segment of the wheel that the spin is expected to land on.
